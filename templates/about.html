Why AirAware Matters

Air pollution is one of the biggest global challenges today. With increasing urbanization and climate changes, understanding the air we breathe is more important than ever. AirAware empowers individuals with clear, reliable, and predictive air quality data — helping you make healthier choices every day.

Air Quality Index (AQI) Categories

0–50 Good Air quality is satisfactory and poses little or no risk.
51–100 Moderate Acceptable; may be a risk for sensitive individuals.
101–150 Unhealthy for Sensitive Groups Sensitive groups may experience effects; general public unlikely to be affected.
151–200 Unhealthy Everyone may begin to feel effects; sensitive groups may feel more serious effects.
201–300 Very Unhealthy Health warnings; everyone should reduce outdoor exertion.
301+ Hazardous Serious health effects; everyone should avoid outdoor activity.
